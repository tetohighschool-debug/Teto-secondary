    More About Teto Secondary School

    Teto has a long-standing reputation for academic resilience.
    Historical Perfomance : In the late 2000s, it was formally recognized by the National Department of Education for "sustained improvement" in Grade 12 results over consecutive years.
    Curriculum : As a public school, it follows the CAPS curriculum, leading to the National Senior Certificate (NSC) in Grade 12.
    Community Role : Being a Quintile 3 school, Teto serves many students from lower-income backgrounds, meaning it does not charge school fees and recieves higher state subsidies to ensure children in the Thabong area have access to quality education. It remains one of the most prominent secondary institutions in the Matjhabeng Local Municipality.
